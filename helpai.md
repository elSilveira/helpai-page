# Prompt: Create the HelpAI Promotional Landing Page

> **Use this prompt to build a high-converting promotional landing page for HelpAI. The page must look like it was designed by a top-tier design agency  NOT an AI-generated template. Think: Linear, Vercel, Raycast, Arc Browser level of craft. Clean typography, intentional whitespace, subtle micro-interactions, dark-mode-first with a premium feel.**

---

## Brand Identity

- **Product Name:** HelpAI
- **Tagline:** "Become the God of Meetings"
- **Secondary Tagline:** "Real-time AI whispering in your ear  invisible to everyone else."
- **Brand Voice:** Confident, direct, slightly bold. Speaks to ambitious professionals who want an unfair advantage. Not corporate fluff  punchy, benefit-driven copy.
- **Color Palette:**
  - Primary Background: Deep dark navy/charcoal (`#0f0f1a` to `#1a1a2e` gradient)
  - Accent: Electric blue (`#89b4fa`) and soft purple (`#cba6f7`)
  - Success/CTA: Vivid green (`#a6e3a1`)
  - Text: Off-white (`#cdd6f4`) on dark, with muted secondary text (`#6c7086`)
  - Danger/urgency pops: Warm coral (`#f38ba8`)
- **Typography:** Inter or Satoshi for body. Cal Sans or a geometric display font for headlines. Generous letter-spacing on headings, tight on body.
- **Design Language:** Dark glassmorphism cards, subtle grain texture on backgrounds, soft glow effects behind key elements, NO generic stock photos. All imagery must feel custom-crafted.

## Global Design Rules

1. **NO AI-generated look.** No gradient blobs slapped onto a white background. No generic SaaS templates. This should look like it was hand-crafted by a senior designer at a top studio.
2. **Dark-mode only.** The entire page is dark. No light mode toggle needed.
3. **Typography hierarchy matters.** Headlines should be 48-72px, bold, with tight letter-spacing. Body text should be 16-18px with generous line-height (1.6-1.8). Use weight contrast (800 for headlines, 400 for body).
4. **Whitespace is a feature.** Sections should breathe. Don't cram content. Let each section have 120-160px vertical padding.
5. **Micro-interactions:** Buttons glow on hover. Cards lift slightly on hover (transform + shadow). Scroll-triggered fade-in animations (subtle, not flashy). Pricing cards tilt slightly on hover (3D perspective).
6. **Glassmorphism cards:** Semi-transparent backgrounds with backdrop blur, thin 1px borders in muted accent colors.
7. **No stock photos.** All visuals should be custom illustrations, 3D renders, or product UI mockups.
8. **Responsive.** Mobile-first responsive design. Stack columns vertically on mobile. Reduce heading sizes proportionally.
9. **Performance.** Lazy-load images. Use WebP format. Optimize for Core Web Vitals.
10. **Accessibility.** All text must meet WCAG AA contrast ratios. Interactive elements must have focus states. Images must have alt text.
